This website highlights the incredible games created by participants during the Hackascratch Hackathon held at PES University, RR Campus. 🎉 Dive in to explore the creativity and innovation of the talented developers! 🚀
About the Hackathon

The Hackascratch Hackathon is an exciting event that brings together aspiring developers to collaborate, learn, and create engaging projects using Scratch. 🤝 Participants had the opportunity to showcase their skills and creativity, resulting in a variety of fun and interactive games. 🎮✨

---

### Features

- 🎮 Interactive Games: Explore a collection of games developed by participants, each showcasing unique gameplay and design.
- 📱 User-Friendly Interface: Navigate easily through the website on PC as well as Mobile to find and play games.
- 💖 Likes & Comments: Participants & Mentors can like and comment on the various games available.
- 🗳️ Voting system: Participants could check out other peoples' games and vote for their favorite one for a community award.
- 🔐 Role based access: Teams, Mentors & Admins had separate dashboards to ensure a seamless experience
